## ⚡ Why this Template?
Most construction websites are slow and hard to use. This template is a **conversion engine** designed with one goal: **Getting you more phone calls and WhatsApp messages.**

### 💎 Premium Features
- **3-Way Conversion Modal:** High-impact contact selector (WhatsApp, Direct Call, and Email).
- **White-Label Architecture:** Change 1 file (`data.js`) and the entire website (SEO, Colors, Services, Brand) updates instantly.
- **Zero-Friction UX:** Optimized for "on-the-job" clients who browse from their phones.
- **High-End Dark Aesthetic:** Premium look that justifies higher rates for your services.
- **No Backend Required:** Works out of the box with WhatsApp API and Mailto protocols.

## 🛠️ Speed-Run Setup (10 Minutes)
1. **Clone & Install**: `npm install`
2. **Personalize**: Open `src/config/data.js` and drop your info (Phone, Name, Services).
3. **Deploy**: Push to Vercel/Netlify for free.
4. **Profit**: Start receiving leads directly to your pocket.
